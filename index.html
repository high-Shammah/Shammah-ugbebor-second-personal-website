My academic journey in Physics and Materials Science laid the foundation for a career that seamlessly blends scientific inquiry with creative design.

Starting my design odyssey in 2020, I embarked on a transformative path from graphic design to specializing in the captivating realm of UI/UX design.

Having collaborated with three different companies and over 10 projects, I've cultivated adaptability and a relentless commitment to achieving project objectives and I posess very strong ability to work with people of different races and genders.

Marrying my design prowess with frontend development skills like HTML5, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, REACT JS, I bridge imagination with functionality to breathe life into captivating web projects.

From conceptualization to realization, I proudly crafted this very website and am currently channeling my energies into another promising digital venture.

Beyond design and code, I've harnessed the power of words as a copywriter, effectively weaving narratives that resonate and engage.

Fuelled by an unending thirst for knowledge, I relentlessly pursue growth opportunities, ensuring I stay on the cutting edge of design, development, and digital innovation.
